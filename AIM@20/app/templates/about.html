About AIM@20

Our Story

Founded by educators and AI researchers, AIM20/VISION20 was born from the frustration of seeing talented students struggle with time management and study organization. We saw an opportunity to combine academic research on learning science with modern AI capabilities to create something truly transformative.

Today, we're proud to serve students from over 50 countries, helping them not just study harder, but study smarter.
